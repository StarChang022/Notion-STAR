High Costs for New Employee Training and Onboarding? Create an SOP to Streamline the Process

2023/06/25

It's Always Troublesome to Arrange New Employee Training

Do you often find it troublesome and time-consuming to arrange training programs when new employees join? Especially when your organization is experiencing rapid expansion or frequent personnel changes, the costs can multiply, and I understand how challenging that can be.

Having to rethink every time, "Didn't we do this before?" or "I remember the relevant information was stored somewhere..." or "I can't recall which courses we had..." is exhausting! It's time to consider designing Standard Operating Procedures (SOP). Doing so will not only help reduce costs but also systematically pass on the organization's experience and knowledge, enabling new hires to adapt and contribute to their work more quickly.

Benefits of Establishing Training SOP

Creating SOP for training allows for the systematic organization of courses, organizational processes, and training materials. This enables team members to easily access the necessary information and ensures that each new hire receives consistent training.

A more apparent advantage is the significant cost reduction. When new employees follow the SOP for training or receive relevant materials, the training arrangements can be completed quickly.

The time and cost saved can be reinvested in further optimizing the training system and improving the quality of the training courses.

How to Create Training SOP Using Notion?

First, create a Gallery List.

To generate a Gallery List, please refer to Essential tools and access the Tools Menu. Select Gallery List from the options provided.

Click on the "More" button at the top and go to Layout. Set the Card Preview to "Page cover." I will personally adjust the Card size to "small" for a better appearance.

Inside the page, click on "Add a property" to add a new property. Create a Multi-select property as "TAG" to classify the knowledge in this article, such as a specific process or tool. Then, go to Properties and change the status of TAGs to "Show.”

At the top of the page, there is an "Add cover" button where you can choose or upload a cover image.

If you prefer not to search for images for each article, you can go back and set the Card Preview to "None.”

This way, you can generate a card-style layout similar to a blog list.

Users can filter the articles they want to read by searching for keywords or selecting specific tags.

If you want to differentiate reading permissions based on departments, you can use the first-level cards as department entrances and set separate reading permissions for each.

For instructions on setting permissions, please refer to the "Sharing is more convenient" section.

If you want users to have a button to click and return to the list after reading an article, you can use the Button feature.

To do this, add a Button and choose the type as "Open page". In the Input section, enter the button name. In the Select page option, choose the Target page you want to link to. Set the opening method as "Full page". This way, you can create a proper link button.

If you want to automatically generate the previously set link button or any other predefined format every time you add a new article, you can set up a Default Template.

Click on the dropdown menu of the NEW button and select New template. Edit the content format you want to appear, and then click the more icon next to the edited Template and select Set as default.

From now on, every time you add a new article, it will automatically include the pre-edited format.

As for editing the content of the articles, it is similar to editing a blog post. If you are unsure about which tools to use, you can refer to the Essential tools for guidance.

On the content page, you can engage in discussions by expanding the "Add a comment" section, which opens up a threaded discussion for the entire page. Alternatively, you can select specific text within a paragraph and click "Comment" to initiate a contextual discussion.

Training SOP can also demonstrate the growth of your organization

In addition to training and knowledge transfer, SOP can serve as important indicators of organizational growth and development. You will pay attention to whether the SOP align with the current situation and identify areas that need improvement. Continuously updating and optimizing them signifies the improvement in both the quality and quantity of your organization.

Notion is well-suited for creating SOP as it allows for easy categorization and searchability. Moreover, it facilitates collaborative discussions and enables you to receive feedback from team members at any time.
