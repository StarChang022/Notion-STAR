Other Settings

OK, you have learned most of the Notion features, let's take a look at what items are in the Settings & members.

  • Author
  • STAR Chang

Most of the basic features of Notion have been covered, and even if you're not familiar with them yet, you should have an idea of the tools available. The only thing left to explain is the left-side Sidebar. Now, let's take a look at the menu and the corresponding functionalities. We'll go through them from top to bottom in order.
  1. Workspace
    The top dropdown menu allows you to select a Workspace. Here, you will see Workspaces that you have created or those to which others have invited you.
  2. Search
    As we mentioned in the Notion Structure section, Notion's hierarchical data structure can expand infinitely. When the scale becomes large enough, it may be difficult for you to find certain pages. In such cases, you can use the Search function to quickly locate pages.
  3. Updates
    All relevant notifications and updates will appear in the Updates section, and a red number will be displayed to alert you.
  4. All teamspaces
    Here, you can manage all your teamspaces or create a new one.
  5. Settings & members
    This option allows you to configure various personal settings, including basic profile information, notification settings, language preferences, Workspace settings, team member management, upgrading Notion plans, and viewing billing information.
  6. Templates
    If you lack inspiration for creating pages, you can choose from the free templates provided by Notion.
  7. Import
    You can directly import documents into pages, and Notion will extract the usable data from the document. Supported document types include Word, Markdown, HTML, and more.
  8. Trash
    Deleted pages are temporarily stored in the Trash section. You can choose to restore or permanently delete pages from there. The Trash section will automatically delete pages that have been stored for a certain period of time, with the retention period depending on your Notion plan.
