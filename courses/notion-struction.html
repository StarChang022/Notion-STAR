Notion struction

Notion can be Structured with unlimited layer similar to a website, extending deeper layer by layer according to your needs.

  • Author
  • STAR Chang

Notion is not just for Note, it can be used to manage everything related to your life. Users can easily create various types of content including text, images, videos, and attachments in Notion and rearrange or organize them as needed.

When you open Notion, you will see a sidebar on the left side containing various Pages. These Pages represent the top-level paths to all your pages. You can click on a Page to navigate to it and then continue opening pages hierarchically within that page.

For example, you can see the example of a top-level "HomePage" that contains a "2nd Page." Inside the "2nd Page," you can further create a "3rd Page," and so on, following this pattern.

HomePage > 2nd Page > 3rd Page > 4th Page > 5th Page ...

The data structure hierarchy in Notion can be expanded indefinitely, allowing you to determine how many levels to unfold based on your specific needs.

Pages in Notion can have various styles, such as List, Gallery, Table, or even formats like Trello boards, Calendar, and Timeline.
